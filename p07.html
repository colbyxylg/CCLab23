Project #7: Auto Bio Game Proposal

Description

Let's Go To The Beach! This will be a game about growing up and learning to be in the world like I did, inspired by a beach near my house. The character that people play is like me as a kid who goes to the beach.

Design Process

My three scenes will be like this. 0. Opening text with 'Lets go to the beach!' 1. Learning to walk on slippery rocks. The player will have to get down to the beach by walking through some slippery rocks. Use arrow keys to move and figure out the best path. Some rocks will be slippery and some won't. Jump over slippery rocks. Slippery rocks look similar to non-slippery rocks - darker rocks are wet. 2. You're at the beach! You see some little crabs scuttling under rocks. You decide to collect all the crabs. You have to catch them! First, you have to make little habitat for crabs: collect sand and rocks first. After all the sand and rocks have been collected, you can then click on the crabs. 3. We are at the beach. Take a polar plunge? Original question: "The ocean water can be nice to swim in. It's pretty cold though. Do you want to take a polar plunge?" Yes or No If yes and if sun is high in the sky, person is like "nice! That was refreshing" If yes sun is low in the sky, person is like "oh, that was cold" If no, "are you sure???" If yes, go to go home screen If no, go to original question 4. Time to go home! Scene fades out.

Stylistically, I plan to use a simple and elegant 2D style. I think you'd move through space like a platformer game like Mario, and it would also be similar to a text-based text game because prompts ask you to do different things.

Keyboard Interactions

I plan on using the arrow keys to move, the space bar to jump, and a mouse click to put the crabs/sand/rocks in a bucket.
