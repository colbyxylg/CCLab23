Project #10: Experimental Camera

Description

It's hard to remember events in distinct, sequential ways. For me, I feel like as soon as the moment has passed it becomes one big memory outline. I tried to represent that in a camera, where the last photo taken blurs out all images, including that last one taken. I was inspired by a traditional strip that you might find in a photobooth.

Below is the strip, where on the 4th image, it is blurred.

Reflection
